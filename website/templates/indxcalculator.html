About Spectral Indices

Spectral indices are combinations of spectral reflectance from two or more wavelengths that indicate relative abundance of features of interest. They are widely used in remote sensing for vegetation monitoring, urban mapping, and environmental assessment.

NDVI Characteristics

  • Range: -1 to +1
  • Healthy vegetation: 0.6 to 0.9
  • Uses NIR and Red bands

NDBI Characteristics

  • Range: -1 to +1
  • Built-up areas: >0.2
  • Uses SWIR and NIR bands

NDWI Characteristics

  • Range: -1 to +1
  • Water bodies: >0.3
  • Uses Green and NIR bands
